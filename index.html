About me

I may have started out as a rocket scientist in the making, but quickly realized that the cutthroat world of engineering wasn't for me. Instead, I found my calling in the creative and strategic fields of design and business.

My journey has taken me from designing logos and freelancing for clients, to building a stock trading app that web scraped stock data from the internet (oh yes, I'm that cool). But it wasn't until I stumbled upon the world of UI/UX design that everything finally clicked.

Now, I'm a full-fledged UX designer with a focus on finance, and I couldn't be happier. I'm constantly learning and discovering new ways to create amazing user experiences, and I can't wait to see where this exciting journey takes me next.
